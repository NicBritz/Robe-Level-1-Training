The Robe Robin® Series

The Robe ROBIN® Series was introduced in 2009 with the 300 and 600 series Spot, Wash and Beam fixtures.

300E Wash

300E Beam

Some AT series and those before used mainly transformer power supplies, magnetic ballasts and dual-in-line PIC (Programmable Integrated Circuit) processors and very basic feature control. However, their simplicity was the reason for their success and Robe products gained a good reputation for reliability.

With the market demanding more in the way of sophistication, Robe responded to this by drawing together advances in switch mode power supplies, microprocessors and reduction in the costs of touchscreen technology to create the ROBIN® Series.

Robe was one of the first manufacturers to incorporate RDM almost from its inception. (All of the above terms will be explained!) But more importantly, Robe introduced technology of their own to make the fixtures brighter, lighter and more versatile. Their design philosophy is summed up in three words;

Smaller, Lighter, Brighter
